About Me Computer Scientist

My name is Bridget Lane and welcome to my homepage. I am an undergraduate Computer Science student studying at George Mason University, currently in my Senior year (Fall 2012-Spring 2016). My interests are in game design, web development, and graphics. I work as an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant in the Computer Science Department here at George Mason, where I assist in intro-level CS classes by working in labs, answering student questions, helping to facilitate quizzes, and monitoring and answering questions on Piazza.

On my spare time I am an avid writer. I enjoy writing for fun, and generally write fiction and science fiction. I am an artist, and specialize realistic pencil sketches, mainly of animals. I also play the violin and quilt.
