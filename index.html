About Me Computer Scientist

I am an undergraduate Computer Science student studying at George Mason University, currently in my Senior year (Fall 2012-Spring 2016). My interests are in game design, web development, and graphics. I work at Gannett Digital and USA Today as part of the Platform as a Service Delivery team in order to help maintain a cloud-based platform focused on automation and continuous integration. I formerly worked as an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant in the Computer Science Department here at George Mason, where I assisted in intro-level CS classes by working in labs, answering student questions, helping to facilitate quizzes, and monitoring and answering questions on Piazza.

The best way to reach me is through my email, which can be found on my CV.
