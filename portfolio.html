The newly ripened cherry trees
We saw amongst the perky grass
We were not the first or the last

From all around they came to watch
Those lovely blossoms and their soft
Little, pink leaves among the knots
Of wooden trees and grassy spots

Oh sweet, small leaves as you do bloom
Reminding us of spring so soon.
